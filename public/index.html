About Us

Founded in 2015, Loom & Luster has established itself as a premier home décor consultancy serving clients across the United States. Our mission is to transform living spaces into personalized sanctuaries that balance aesthetic appeal with functional design.

We believe that every home tells a story, and our team of experienced designers works closely with you to craft a narrative that reflects your unique taste and lifestyle. From cozy apartments to expansive estates, we approach each project with the same dedication to quality and attention to detail.

Our philosophy centers on the harmonious blend of texture (loom) and light (luster) to create spaces that feel both inviting and inspiring. We stay at the forefront of design trends while respecting timeless principles that ensure your home remains beautiful for years to come.
