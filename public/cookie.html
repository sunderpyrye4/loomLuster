Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2023

This Cookie Policy explains how Loom & Luster uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which we collectively refer to as "cookies" in this policy.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Examples Duration
Essential Cookies Required for website functionality (session management, security) Session ID, authentication tokens Session or up to 24 hours
Analytics Cookies Collect information about how visitors use our website Google Analytics cookies Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Track visitors across websites for targeted advertising Facebook Pixel, Google Ads cookies Up to 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences Language preferences, consent choices Up to 1 year
Performance Cookies Monitor website performance and user experience Load balancing, error tracking Up to 30 days

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance measurement. Google's Cookie Policy
  • Google Ads: For advertising and remarketing purposes. Google Ads Policy
  • Facebook Pixel: For social media advertising and analytics. Facebook Cookie Policy
  • Hotjar: For user experience analysis (optional). Hotjar Cookie Information
  • YouTube: For embedded videos on our site. YouTube Cookies

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We encourage you to review them.

4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept All: Accept all cookies (essential, analytics, marketing)
  • Reject All: Reject all non-essential cookies
  • Customize: Choose which cookie categories to accept

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for website functionality. Your consent preferences are stored for 12 months.

Withdrawing Consent: You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all sites
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

Here are links to cookie management for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Our Cookie Preference Center

Accessible via the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer or cookie consent banner.

Third-Party Opt-Outs

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (US)

6. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers. However, you can control tracking through our cookie consent manager and browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing the cookie consent banner again if changes affect your choices

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
