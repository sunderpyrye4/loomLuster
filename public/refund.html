Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2023

We strive for complete client satisfaction. This policy outlines our refund and cancellation procedures.

1. Consultation Fees

Initial consultation fees are non-refundable once the consultation has taken place. If you cancel with more than 48 hours notice, we will issue a full refund of any prepaid consultation fee.

Consultation fees are typically $150 for a 90-minute initial consultation, which is applied toward any future services if you choose to work with us.

2. Service Retainers

Retainer fees paid to secure our services and begin design work are non-refundable, as they cover initial design development, research, and administrative setup.

If a project is cancelled after the retainer has been paid but before substantial work has begun, we may refund a portion at our discretion, minus any costs incurred (typically 50% of retainer).

Example: If you pay a $2,000 retainer and cancel before we begin design work, we may refund $1,000 to cover our preliminary administrative costs.

3. Project Cancellation

If you choose to cancel a project after work has begun:

  • You will be billed for all work completed up to the cancellation date at our standard hourly rate
  • Any unused portion of prepaid fees will be refunded within 30 days
  • All intellectual property rights remain with Loom & Luster until full payment is received for work completed
  • A cancellation fee of 10% of remaining project value may apply

4. Product Purchases

For furniture and accessories purchased through us:

Product Type Return Policy Restocking Fee
Custom orders Non-refundable, non-returnable N/A
Stock items 14-day return window 15%
Special orders Case by case basis 20-50%
Damaged/defective items Replacement or full refund None

All returns must be in original packaging and condition. Shipping costs for returns are the client's responsibility unless the item is defective.

5. Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on our design services:

  • If you're unsatisfied with our design concepts, we will revise them once at no additional charge
  • If after revisions you remain unsatisfied, we will refund 50% of fees paid for the design development phase
  • This guarantee does not apply to implemented work, product purchases, or travel expenses
  • Satisfaction guarantee claims must be made within 14 days of concept delivery

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us at billing@loomandluster.com within 30 days of payment
  2. Include your invoice number and reason for refund request
  3. Allow 5-10 business days for processing
  4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method

Processing Time: Refunds typically appear in your account within 7-10 business days after processing, depending on your financial institution.

7. Exceptions

No refunds will be issued for:

  • Services already rendered
  • Digital products (e-books, design guides)
  • Travel expenses already incurred
  • Third-party vendor fees (architectural drawings, contractor deposits)
  • Projects cancelled due to client failure to provide necessary information or access
