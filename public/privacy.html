Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Loom & Luster collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address when you contact us or book a consultation.
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment details when you purchase our services.
  • Communications: Records of your communications with us, including emails and contact form submissions.
  • Project Information: Details about your home, design preferences, and project requirements.

We also automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our website:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website, and browsing actions.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Purpose Legal Basis
Providing and improving our décor consultancy services Performance of contract
Communicating with you about appointments, updates, and offers Legitimate interest
Processing payments and sending invoices Performance of contract
Personalizing your experience on our website Legitimate interest
Analyzing website usage and improving our marketing Legitimate interest
Complying with legal obligations Legal compliance

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf (payment processing, email delivery, hosting).
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly consent to the sharing.

Third-Party Services: Our website uses Google Analytics to analyze traffic. Google may collect and process data according to their privacy policy. We also use Google Ads for advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Client information: 7 years after last service (for tax and legal purposes)
  • Marketing data: Until you opt-out or request deletion
  • Website analytics: 26 months
  • Financial records: 7 years (as required by law)

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
